About Pakistan Meteorological Department (PMD)

The Pakistan Meteorological Department (PMD) is an autonomous and independent institution established in 1947. PMD is responsible for weather forecasting, climate monitoring, meteorological research, and issuing public weather warnings for the safety and protection of people across Pakistan.

PMD plays an important role in aviation, agriculture, disaster management, water resources, and climate studies by providing accurate and timely weather information.

Internship Opportunities

PMD offers internship opportunities for undergraduate students interested in Software Development, Information Technology, Data Science, Weather Data Analysis, and Research.

Interns gain practical experience by working on real-world projects under the supervision of experienced professionals.
